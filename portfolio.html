About Me

The person
behind the page

I'm Sidharth, a Mumbai-based freelance writer working at the intersection of culture, technology, and the quietly extraordinary. My work has appeared in The Hindu, Mint Lounge, The Wire, and several international publications.

I came to writing through philosophy — a roundabout path that left me perpetually obsessed with the questions underneath things. I'm drawn to stories that resist easy answers, and to the kind of sentence that makes you re-read it twice.

When I'm not writing, I'm probably wandering a city I don't know well enough, arguing about cinema, or reading something I'll recommend to people who didn't ask.
